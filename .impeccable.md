## Design Context

### Users
Individual readers who store web novels. Core tasks: folder organization, reading text, importing external links, and resuming from the last position.

### Brand Personality
Quiet, focused, and calming. Drive-like clarity with a reading-first mindset.

### Aesthetic Direction
Google Drive?inspired structure combined with an immersive web-novel reader. Support both light and dark modes.

### Design Principles
1. Drive-like information architecture; reader-like immersion.
2. Fast, predictable interactions with immediate feedback.
3. Clean, minimal visuals with no decorative noise.
4. Readability-first in both light and dark themes.
